What we move

One partner for every load size.

From a single pallet to a full truck, Spera Logistics gives you flexible, reliable freight options backed by a connected pan-India network.

PTL — Part Truck Load

Pay only for the space you use. Ideal for SMEs and regular shippers needing cost-effective, scheduled freight.

FTL — Full Truck Load

Dedicated vehicles for high-volume moves. Direct, point-to-point delivery with priority transit times.

Surface Cargo

Economical road cargo for bulky and non-urgent consignments across our nationwide line-haul lanes.

Door-to-Door

We pick up from your dock and deliver to the consignee's doorstep — no middle handling, no hassle.

Corporate Logistics

Managed freight programs for enterprises — dedicated dashboards, account support and SLA-backed delivery.

E-commerce B2B

Scalable first- and middle-mile delivery built for online sellers, distributors and retail supply chains.
